About

Whistle Clean Janitorial LLC is a licensed, insured, and family owned commercial cleaning business based in Douglasville, Georgia. We are very passionate about making your working environment a clean and healthy place to conduct your business, so that you and your customers will be happy at all times.

Services Offered:

We specialize in janitorial services and hard floor mainteance. some of the our services include;

  • General and Detailed Office Cleaning
  • Break Room Cleaning
  • Bathroom Sanitation
  • Window Washing
  • Carpet Cleaning and Maintenance
  • Post Construction Cleaning
  • Post Event Cleaning

Industries We Serve

We provide after hours and commercial cleaning services to the following;

  • Gyms

  • Churches
  • Schools
  • HOA Clubhouses
  • Banks
  • Retaurants
